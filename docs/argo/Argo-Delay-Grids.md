How to understand the Delay Grid chart for Argo:

The two tables display the statistics on data delay for the two GDAC.
The rows refers to the DAC
The columns refers to the different delay statistics

* Average(h): Average time between observation (i.e. Argo float surfacing) and data availability at GDAC
* Median(h): Median time between observation (i.e. Argo float surfacing) and data availability at GDAC
* % on target: **_§§§§§_**
* DAC Average(h): Average time between observation (i.e. Argo float surfacing) and data availability at DAC
* GDAC Average(h): Average time between data availability at DAC and data availability at GDAC
* DAC Median(h): Median time between observation (i.e. Argo float surfacing) and data availability at DAC
* GDAC Median(h): Median time between data availability at DAC and data availability at GDAC
* #included observations: Number of observations used for the computation
* #ignored observations: Number of observations excluded for the computation. Those observations are excluded **_§§§§§_**
